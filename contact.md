# Contact & Support

Get help with the AfriGen-D Imputation Service. Our team is here to assist with technical issues, scientific questions, and collaboration opportunities.

## Support Channels

### Technical Support

For issues with imputation jobs, data preparation, or technical questions:

**Email**: [support@imputationserver.com](mailto:support@imputationserver.com)
**Response Time**: Within 24 hours
**Available**: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM EST

For questions about methodology, interpretation, or research applications:

**Email**: [science@imputationserver.com](mailto:science@imputationserver.com)

## How to Get Help

### For Technical Issues

When reporting technical problems, please include:

- **Job ID** (if applicable)
- **Error messages** or screenshots
- **Data description**: File format, sample size, population
- **Steps you've already tried**

**Example Support Request:**

```
Subject: Job Failure - ID: job_123456

Hello,

My imputation job (ID: job_123456) failed during the QC phase.

Data details:
- File format: bgzip-compressed VCF (.vcf.gz)
- Sample size: 5,000 individuals
- Population: West African (Nigerian)
- Reference panel: H3Africa v6
- Genome build: GRCh38

Error message: "Insufficient variants passing QC filters"

I've validated my VCF files with vcf-validator and they pass.
Could you help identify the issue?

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Institution]
```

### For Scientific Questions

When asking about methodology or interpretation:

- **Research question** or hypothesis
- **Study population** and design
- **Specific analysis goals**
- **Relevant context** about your project
