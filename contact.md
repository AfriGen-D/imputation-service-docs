## Getting Help

### Before Contacting Support

Please try these steps first:

1. **Search our documentation** using the search bar above
2. **Check the FAQ page**: [Genotype Imputation FAQ](genotype-imputation/faq.md)
3. **Review the troubleshooting guides** in our [Resources](resources.md) section
4. **Check our status page** for any ongoing issues

### What to Include in Your Message

To help us assist you quickly, please include:

**For Technical Issues:**
- Your job ID (if applicable)
- Error messages or screenshots
- Description of your data (file format, sample size, population)
- Steps you've already tried

**For Scientific Questions:**
- Your research question or hypothesis
- Study population and design
- Specific analysis goals
- Any relevant publications or methods

**Example Support Request:**
```
Subject: Job Failure - ID: job_123456

Hello,

My imputation job (ID: job_123456) failed during the QC phase. 

Data details:
- File format: VCF
- Sample size: 5,000 individuals
- Population: European ancestry
- Array: Illumina Global Screening Array

Error message: "Insufficient variants passing QC filters"

I've already checked my data preparation following your guidelines,
and the input file validated successfully with vcf-validator.

Could you help me understand what might be causing this issue?

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```
